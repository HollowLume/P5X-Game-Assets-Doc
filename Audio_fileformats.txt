P5X\client\bin\Media\Windows is where the audio files are. Fileformats -[.acb]-[.awb]-[[.usm]
___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
.awb (Adaptive Waveform Binary)
Stores compressed or uncompressed audio data that .acb references Once extracted, you might get audio in formats like WAV or ADX.
____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
.acb (Audio Cue Binary)
Holds multiple audio files (like music tracks, sound effects, or voice lines) along with metadata.
____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
.usm (CRI Movie)
Stores video cutscenes, often with audio and subtitles embedded, using a proprietary compression method.
